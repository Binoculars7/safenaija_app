About SafeNaija

It detects the type of incident & sends it to the right agency.

SafeNaija helps people report problems like robbery, fire, accidents, or any emergency. When you report an issue, the app uses smart technology (AI) to understand what kind of problem it is. Then it quickly sends your report to the right agency, like the police, fire service, or ambulance, so they can come and help fast.

In short, SafeNaija makes it easy for Nigerians to get help quickly when something bad happens.

Frequently Asked Questions

SafeNaija is an emergency response platform that helps Nigerian citizens quickly report emergencies and get help. When you report an incident, our system immediately notifies the appropriate emergency services (police, fire service, or ambulance) closest to your location for rapid response.

Response times vary depending on your location and the type of emergency. However, our system ensures the fastest possible response by automatically alerting the nearest emergency service unit. In urban areas, response times typically range from 5-15 minutes.

Yes, SafeNaija is available across all 36 states of Nigeria and the FCT. We work with local emergency services in each state to ensure comprehensive coverage, though response capabilities may vary by location.

While our app works best with internet connectivity, we have implemented SMS fallback options for areas with poor internet coverage. This ensures you can still report emergencies even with limited connectivity.

Absolutely! At SafeNaija, we take your privacy and security very seriously. All reports and personal details are encrypted and shared only with the relevant emergency authorities to provide you with the best and swiftest response possible. We never sell or misuse your information.

SafeNaija is designed primarily for reporting emergencies such as crimes, accidents, fire outbreaks, or medical incidents requiring urgent attention. For less urgent matters, we encourage you to contact the appropriate local authorities directly, but you can still leave a report and we will direct it to the right agency.

After you report an incident on SafeNaija, you will receive real-time updates through your dashboard or via SMS/email notifications if you choose. You can log in anytime to check the update status of your report and view feedback from responding agencies.

We welcome partnerships and collaborations with organizations, agencies, and individuals who share our mission of making Nigeria safer. If you wish to support or work with SafeNaija, please visit our Contact page or email us at support@safenaija.com.
